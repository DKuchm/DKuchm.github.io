Privacy Policy

MyEasyFarm does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All data is stored locally on the user's device using Apple's CoreData framework.

The app may use the device camera only for scanning animal ear tags (QR or barcode). No images or data are stored or transmitted.

No third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.
